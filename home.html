Welcome to Cyber Security Center Website

The Cybersecurity Center for Research, Education, and Outreach (CCERO) at UM-D is established in Summer 2016 with an effort to integrate university wide existing activities and initiatives in cybersecurity research, education, and outreach. It is dedicated to facilitate faculty members of diverse disciplines for cutting-edge research collaboration, provide high quality degree and certificate programs, advise student groups, and organize events in the area of cyber security and information assurance.

The center is helping to address the national concern for producing more cyber security professionals and advancing the knowledge and practice of cybersecurity and digital forensics through research, education, and outreach.
