Undergraduate Education

•Bachelor of Science in Digital Forensics is offered through the Department of Computer and Information Science of College of Engineering and Computer Science.

•Undergraduate Certificate Program in Practical Aspects of Computer Security is offered through the Extended Learning and Outreach Center of College of Engineering and Computer Science.

•Starting from the fall of 2017, we will be offering a BS in Cyber Security and Information Assurance through the Department of Computer and Information Science with two concentrations: (1) Cyber Security; (2) Digital Forensics.

Graduate Education

•Masters of Science in Computer and Information Science with a concentration on Computer Networks and Security is offered through the Department of Computer and Information Science of College of Engineering and Computer Science.

•Ph.D. in Information Systems Engineering with focus on cyber security research is offered through a joint interdisciplinary program between the CIS, ECE, and IMSE departments.

•Starting from the fall of 2016, Ph.D. with a focus on Cyber Security will be offered by individual home department.
